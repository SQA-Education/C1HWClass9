1. Conversation Awareness

Continuing Your Education in the United States

Many adults in the United States are continuing their education while working full-time. Community colleges and universities offer programs that help immigrants improve their English, earn a degree, or start a new career path.

Every student's journey is different. Some return to school after many years to pursue a long-held goal. Others face challenges like tuition costs, language barriers, or balancing work and family responsibilities.

Academic advisors, tutors, and financial aid programs can help students succeed and reach their educational goals.
